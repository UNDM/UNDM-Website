Ways to Sponsor UNDM

Monetary donations are versatile as they can go towards operating costs of HuskerThon and/or our grand fundraising goal. Monetary Donation

In-Kind Donation

In-kind donations are greatly appreciated as they can go toward silent auction, fundraising incentives, and offsetting event costs.

Event Catering

With HuskerThon spanning over the course of 12 hours, we provide food to all of our particpants. Meals, drinks, and snacks are greatly appreciated. We often need food donations for community events throughout the year.

Host a Percentage Night

Partner with us during a "fundraising push day" to provide an hour of the day whree all donations made to UNDM are matched by an amount of your choosing.

Donation Matching Hour

Partner with us for a day and give a portion of your proceeds to UNDM or advertise for a specific product that customers can enjoy.
