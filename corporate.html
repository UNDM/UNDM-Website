Ways to Sponsor UNDM

Monetary Donation

Monetary donations are versatile as they can go towards operating costs of HuskerThon and/or our grand fundraising goal.

In-Kind Donation

In-kind donations are greatly appreciated as they can go toward silent auction, fundraising incentives, and offsetting event costs.

Catering the Event

We provide food at our 12 hour long HuskerThon and various community events. Donations of meals, drinks, and snacks are greatly appreciated.

Host a Percentage Night

Partner with us during a "fundraising push day" to provide an hour of the day where all donations made to UNDM are matched by an amount of your choosing.

Donation Matching Hour

Partner with us for a day and give a portion of your proceeds to UNDM or advertise for a specific product that customers can enjoy.
