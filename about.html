Our Purpose

University of Nebraska Dance Marathon exists to raise funds and increase awareness for Children’s Hospital & Medical Center in Omaha, Nebraska through uniting Husker Nation to ensure every child has the ability to receive the highest quality care.

Our Mission

To cultivate student leaders, foster campus community, and unite Husker Nation to champion kids' health

Our Vision

To ensure every child has the opportunity to become a Husker

Meet our Executive Team!

Ashley McMurchie

Executive Director/President

The President of UNDM serves as the liaison between Dance Marathon and UNL. Running mostly behind the scenes, the president makes sure her team of hard-working, passionate individuals has a smooth year.

Ashley has been making a difference with Dance Marathon for 4 years. Her favorite memory is her first bracelet cutting ceremony or being Elliot's family rep! The bracelet cutting ceremony hit home and solidified to her why UNDM raises money and awareness for the Children's Hospital. Being Elliot's family representative was also rewarding because she spent all of HuskerThon 2020 with him and was able to see the joy this event brings our Miracle Kids.
