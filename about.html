Involvement Roles

Dance Marathon has many different levels of involvement, but all are greatly integrated and necessary for our success.

Executive
Team

The executive team is made up of 21 directors in charge of their respective committees within the organization. These individuals make decisions that enhance all aspects of our mission, from recruitment to event operations.

Staff

Staff make up each committee and complete various tasks that aid in efforts completed leading up to the event. They staff booths and various events held on campus throughout the year, as well as assisting in accomplishing their committee’s goals to improve DM.

Morale
Captains

Morale Captains act as the liaisons between Dance Marathon and Team Captains of other organizations on campus that support our mission. This group is known to set an enthusiastic tone at HuskerThon and boost morale throughout the event.

Dancers

Dancers are students throughout campus that have raised $100 throughout the year and come together the day of the event to help celebrate the year’s accomplishments.

Donors

Lastly, donors are individuals that have supported our organization’s goal to raise money for Children’s Hospital in Omaha. All roles allow us to succeed in our goals of fundraising and building relationships throughout our community.
