Marlys

Marlys suffers from Zygomycosis, BPD, and pulmonary hypertension. Despite being born at 25 weeks, and being given a 20% chance to live, Marlys is now three years old! Her team at Children's has become like family!

Zalyn

Zalyn was born with Trisomy 18 (Edward's Syndrome). Only 5 out of 100 children with Edward's Syndrome make it to birth, and only 1 of those 5 children will make it to their first birthday. Zalyn is now five and a half years old! He loves paw patrol, swimming, and playing outside.

Milly

Milly has dealt with pulmonary hypoplasia and several other conditions in her life. Despite hearing loss brought on by complications with her lungs, Milly has a beautiful singing voice and was named the National Miracle Child in 2017!
